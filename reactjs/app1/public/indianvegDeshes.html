Top 5 Indian Veg Dishes

Paneer Butter Masala

A rich and creamy curry made with soft paneer cubes cooked in a buttery tomato gravy.

Ingredients: Paneer, Butter, Tomatoes, Cream, Spices

Aloo Gobi

A dry curry of potatoes and cauliflower, seasoned with Indian spices.

Ingredients: Potato, Cauliflower, Onion, Tomato, Spices

Palak Paneer

Soft paneer cubes cooked in a healthy spinach gravy with aromatic spices.

Ingredients: Spinach, Paneer, Onion, Garlic, Spices

Dal Makhani

A creamy and buttery black lentil curry, slow-cooked for maximum flavor.

Ingredients: Black Lentils, Kidney Beans, Butter, Cream, Spices

Chana Masala

Spicy chickpea curry, popular across North India, served with rice or bread.

Ingredients: Chickpeas, Onion, Tomato, Ginger, Spices
