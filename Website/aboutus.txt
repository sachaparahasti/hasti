12+ Years of Crafting Quality Construction Essentials

Since 2012, Diyan Enterprises has been at the forefront of manufacturing a wide array of construction essentials, ranging from rubber-molded paver blocks and kerbstones to steel-molded paver blocks, wall cladding tiles, breeze block mesh, and more. Located strategically near Shiv Kunj Ashram on Talaja Main Road, Adhewada, Bhavnagar, we have established ourselves as a reliable partner in the construction and infrastructure industry.

Our products are meticulously crafted using a blend of premium materials including sand, cement, gravel, colorants, chemicals, and lacquer, ensuring unparalleled quality and durability that stands the test of time.
Our Growth Journey

From humble beginnings where our factory production spanned between 500-700 square feet, we've grown exponentially over the years.

Today, our factory boasts a production capacity of 2200-2500 square feet, a testament to our unwavering commitment to excellence and innovation.
