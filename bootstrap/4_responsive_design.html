1. India Launches Solar Mission

ISRO has launched a new mission to study the Sun’s atmosphere. This mission will help scientists understand solar flares and their effects on Earth. Thousands witnessed the launch at Sriharikota. The satellite will orbit the Sun for 5 years, collecting valuable data. It is a proud moment for India’s space program.

2. Gujarat Flood Crisis

Heavy rainfall has caused major flooding in Gujarat. Many homes are submerged, and rescue operations are underway. The government has opened relief camps for affected families. People are advised to stay indoors. Weather forecasts show more rain is expected in the next few days.

3. India in Cricket World Cup Final

India defeated Australia to reach the World Cup final. Virat Kohli scored a brilliant century. Fans celebrated across the country. The final match will be played in Mumbai. India is now one step closer to winning the championship.

4. New Education Policy

The government has launched a new education policy to improve student learning. Coding will be taught from Class 6. Regional languages and digital tools are also promoted. Teachers will get training to adapt to the changes. The policy starts next year.

5. Global Warming Alert

Global temperatures have reached new highs, causing more heatwaves and floods. The UN has asked countries to act quickly to cut emissions. India is investing more in renewable energy. Citizens are urged to use eco-friendly products and reduce plastic waste.

6. Digital India Expands

The Digital India campaign now covers more rural areas. Internet access, online banking, and e-learning are improving. New towers and fiber lines are being installed. The government is also training youth in digital skills to boost job opportunities.
